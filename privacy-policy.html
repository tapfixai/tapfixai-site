Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 13, 2026
App Name: TapFix AI: Text Fix & Translate
Website: tapfixai.app
Support Email: support@tapfixai.app

TapFix AI respects your privacy and is designed to help users improve, correct, rewrite, and translate text more conveniently.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the TapFix AI app may access, how that information is used, and what choices users have when using the app.

By using TapFix AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. What TapFix AI Does

TapFix AI is a productivity tool that helps users work with text faster. The app may provide features such as correcting spelling and grammar, improving writing style, translating selected text, rewriting text in a clearer way, helping users edit text in supported apps, displaying a floating AI button for quick text actions, and replacing selected or typed text after user confirmation.

TapFix AI is not designed for surveillance, tracking, spying, hidden data collection, or unauthorized access to private information.

2. Information the App May Access

TapFix AI may access only the information needed to provide its core features. Depending on how the user uses the app, TapFix AI may access selected text, typed text in supported input fields, text sent for correction, rewriting, or translation, app interface information needed to detect editable text fields, basic app settings, and subscription or purchase status if paid features are used.

The app does not intentionally collect passwords, banking credentials, payment card numbers, private security codes, or other sensitive authentication data.

Users should not send passwords, financial credentials, private keys, medical records, or other highly sensitive information for AI processing.

3. How TapFix AI Uses Text Data

TapFix AI uses text only to perform the action requested by the user. For example, when the user taps the AI button and chooses an action, the app may process the text to correct errors, improve grammar, translate the text, rewrite the text, make the text shorter or clearer, or prepare a replacement result for the input field.

The app does not use accessed text to monitor the user’s behavior. The app does not use Accessibility Service to collect data in the background for advertising, profiling, analytics, or surveillance.

4. Accessibility Service Usage

TapFix AI uses Android Accessibility Service to make the floating AI button and text editing features work across supported apps.

Accessibility permission may be used to detect when the user is typing in an editable text field, show the floating AI button near text input areas, read selected or typed text only when the user requests an AI action, insert corrected, translated, or rewritten text back into the input field, and improve text editing accessibility and productivity.

TapFix AI does not use Accessibility Service to read passwords, collect banking information, monitor private conversations without user action, track user activity across apps, control the device without user permission, perform hidden actions, or collect data for advertising or profiling.

Accessibility permission is required only because Android does not provide another reliable way for an app to assist with text selection, text replacement, and floating AI actions across different apps. Users can disable Accessibility permission at any time in Android Settings.

5. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

Some TapFix AI features may require sending user-provided text to external AI service providers for processing. This may happen when the user chooses actions such as Fix text, Translate, Rewrite, Improve text, Change tone, or Summarize text.

The text is sent only to generate the requested result. TapFix AI may use third-party AI providers, cloud infrastructure, payment providers, analytics tools, or crash reporting services where necessary to operate and improve the app. These services may process limited data required to provide their functionality. TapFix AI does not sell users’ personal data.

6. Data Storage

TapFix AI is designed to minimize data storage. The app may store user settings, selected app preferences, theme settings, menu size settings, language preferences, subscription status, and limited technical logs needed to diagnose errors.

TapFix AI does not intentionally store full user conversations, private messages, passwords, or banking information. Text sent for AI processing may be temporarily processed by AI service providers according to their own security and processing practices.

7. Data Sharing

TapFix AI does not sell personal data. TapFix AI may share limited data only when necessary to provide AI processing, process subscriptions or payments, maintain app security, fix bugs and crashes, comply with legal obligations, or operate required infrastructure.

TapFix AI does not share Accessibility data with advertisers and does not use Accessibility data for ad targeting.

8. Permissions Used by the App

  • Accessibility Service: used to detect editable text fields, show the floating AI button, read selected text after user action, and replace text with the AI-generated result.
  • Display Over Other Apps: used to show the floating AI button above supported apps.
  • Internet Access: used to connect to AI services, subscription services, and app backend systems.
  • Notification Permission: may be used to show important app status messages, updates, or service information.

The app requests permissions only when they are needed for active features.

9. User Control

Users remain in control of their data and permissions. Users can enable or disable Accessibility permission, enable or disable overlay permission, choose which apps TapFix AI works with, stop using AI actions at any time, avoid sending sensitive text for AI processing, uninstall the app at any time, and contact support with privacy questions.

10. Children’s Privacy

TapFix AI is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. The app is designed as a productivity tool for users who need text correction, rewriting, and translation features. TapFix AI does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Security

TapFix AI uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no digital service can guarantee complete security. Users should avoid submitting highly sensitive information such as passwords, banking credentials, private keys, or confidential legal, financial, or medical information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

TapFix AI may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be published on this page with a new effective date. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
