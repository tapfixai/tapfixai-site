Permissions, in plain language.

The keyboard asks for access only to power visible features. You can deny optional permissions, disable features or uninstall the app at any time.

Privacy Policy Data use
Keyboard service: lets Android use TapFix AI Keyboard as your input method.
Internet: connects to TapFix AI servers for user-requested AI actions.
Microphone: enables dictation when you tap the microphone button.
Photos and images: shows recent screenshots/images for quick paste.
Clipboard: stores copied text and image references locally for clipboard history.
Vibration: provides haptic feedback while typing.
